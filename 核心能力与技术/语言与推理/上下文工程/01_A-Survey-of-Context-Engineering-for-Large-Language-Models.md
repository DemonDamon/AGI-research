# A Survey of Context Engineering for Large Language Models

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.13334](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.13334)

## 摘要

A comprehensive survey that formally establishes Context Engineering as a systematic discipline for designing, optimizing, and managing the information payload of Large Language Models (LLMs). It analyzes over 1400 research papers.
