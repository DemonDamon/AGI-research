# Chain-of-Thought Prompting Elicits Reasoning in Large Language Models

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.11903](https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.11903)

## 摘要

The seminal paper introducing Chain-of-Thought (CoT) prompting, a technique that significantly improves the ability of large language models to perform complex reasoning tasks by generating a series of intermediate reasoning steps.
