# Aligning Cyber Space with Physical World: A Comprehensive Survey on Embodied AI

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.06886](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.06886)

## 摘要

A comprehensive survey on Embodied AI, which is crucial for achieving Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) and serves as a foundation for applications that bridge cyberspace and the physical world. It explores embodied perception, interaction, agent design, and sim-to-real adaptation, highlighting the role of Multi-modal Large Models (MLMs) and World Models (WMs).
