# Advancing Spatial Reasoning in Large Language Models

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://ojs.aaai.org/index.php/AAAI/article/view/29811](https://ojs.aaai.org/index.php/AAAI/article/view/29811)

## 摘要

A 2024 paper from the AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence that explores the limits and potential of Large Language Models in spatial reasoning, analyzing their performance on a rectified benchmark and identifying limitations in multi-hop reasoning.
