# A Survey of Large Language Model-Powered Spatial Intelligence Across Scales: Advances in Embodied Agents, Smart Cities, and Earth Science

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.09848](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.09848)

## 摘要

A comprehensive survey on the advancements of Large Language Models (LLMs) in spatial intelligence, covering applications from embodied agents to smart cities and earth science. It reviews human spatial cognition, spatial memory, and knowledge representations in LLMs.
