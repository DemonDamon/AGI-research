# A Survey on Multimodal Large Language Models

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.13549](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.13549)

## 摘要

A comprehensive survey tracing and summarizing the recent progress of Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs), covering architecture, training strategy, data, evaluation, and future directions. Accepted for publication in National Science Review.
