# Knowledge Editing for Large Language Models: A Survey

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.16218](https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.16218)

## 摘要

A comprehensive survey providing an in-depth overview of recent advances in Knowledge Editing (KE) for Large Language Models (LLMs), which is a key method for updating knowledge without full retraining. It covers various editing strategies and challenges.
