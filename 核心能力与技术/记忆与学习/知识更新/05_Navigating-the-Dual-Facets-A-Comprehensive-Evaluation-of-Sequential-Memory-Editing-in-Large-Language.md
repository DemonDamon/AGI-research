**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.11122](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.11122)

## 摘要

A research paper focusing on Memory Editing (ME) as an efficient method to modify or inject new facts into LLMs, specifically evaluating the general capabilities of memory-edited LLMs in sequential editing scenarios.
