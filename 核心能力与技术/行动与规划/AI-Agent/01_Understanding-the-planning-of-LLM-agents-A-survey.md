# Understanding the planning of LLM agents: A survey

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.02716](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.02716)

## 摘要

A systematic survey providing a taxonomy of existing works on LLM-Agent planning, covering Task Decomposition, Plan Selection, External Module, Reflection, and Memory. This is a highly cited paper in the field.
