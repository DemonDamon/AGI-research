# Scaling Laws Do Not Scale

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.03201](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.03201)

## 摘要

A paper from AIES 2024 that critically interrogates the claims of scaling laws, arguing that the relationship between scale and performance is flawed because it relies on metrics that may not align with how different communities perceive model quality, advocating for a resistance to the 'impetus towards scale in AI'.
