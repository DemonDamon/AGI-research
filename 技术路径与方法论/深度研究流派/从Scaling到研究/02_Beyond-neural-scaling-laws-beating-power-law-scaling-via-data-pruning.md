# Beyond neural scaling laws: beating power law scaling via data pruning

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2206.14486](https://arxiv.org/abs/2206.14486)

## 摘要

An Outstanding Paper Award winner at NeurIPS 2022, this paper explores methods like data pruning to break beyond the limitations of power law scaling, suggesting that improved data efficiency through research can substantially reduce the resource costs of modern deep learning and achieve better performance.
