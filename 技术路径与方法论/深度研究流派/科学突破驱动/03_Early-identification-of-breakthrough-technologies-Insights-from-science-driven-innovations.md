# Early identification of breakthrough technologies: Insights from science-driven innovations

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1751157724001184](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1751157724001184)

## 摘要

A research paper that proposes a method to identify breakthrough technologies early by constructing a science-technology knowledge network and using a two-stage selection method to funnel scientific innovation signals. It validates the method with a use case in artificial intelligence.
