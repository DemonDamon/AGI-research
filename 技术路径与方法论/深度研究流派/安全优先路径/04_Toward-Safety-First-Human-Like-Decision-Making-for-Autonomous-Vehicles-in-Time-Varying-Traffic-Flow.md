# Toward Safety-First Human-Like Decision Making for Autonomous Vehicles in Time-Varying Traffic Flow

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/html/2506.14502v1](https://arxiv.org/html/2506.14502v1)

## 摘要

An academic paper proposing a Safety-First Human-Like Decision Making (SF-HLDM) framework for autonomous vehicles, which integrates a hierarchical progressive framework to balance safety, efficiency, comfort, and social compliance in complex traffic environments.
