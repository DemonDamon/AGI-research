# What is AI safety? What do we want it to be?

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11098-025-02367-z](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11098-025-02367-z)

## 摘要

A philosophical and conceptual analysis of AI safety, arguing for the 'Safety Conception' which defines AI safety as the inquiry aimed at preventing or mitigating harms from AI development and deployment, and advocating for its use in research prioritization.
