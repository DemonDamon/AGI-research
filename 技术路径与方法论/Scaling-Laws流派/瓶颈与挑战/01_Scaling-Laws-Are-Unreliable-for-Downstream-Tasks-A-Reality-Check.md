# Scaling Laws Are Unreliable for Downstream Tasks: A Reality Check

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.00885](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.00885)

## 摘要

A meta-analysis of existing data on downstream scaling laws, finding that close fit to linear scaling laws only occurs in a minority of cases (39%). It highlights that scaling laws are context-specific and often unreliable, with phenomena like emergence and inverse scaling being common.
