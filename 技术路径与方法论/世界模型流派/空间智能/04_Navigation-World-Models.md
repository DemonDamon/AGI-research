# Navigation World Models

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2025/html/Bar_Navigation_World_Models_CVPR_2025_paper.html](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2025/html/Bar_Navigation_World_Models_CVPR_2025_paper.html)

## 摘要

A CVPR 2025 paper introducing a Navigation World Model (NWM), a controllable video generation model that predicts future visual observations based on past observations and navigation actions, demonstrating its effectiveness for planning in both familiar and unfamiliar environments.
