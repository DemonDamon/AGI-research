# Building spatial world models from sparse transitional episodic memories

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/html/2505.13696v1](https://arxiv.org/html/2505.13696v1)

## 摘要

A research paper proposing the Episodic Spatial World Model (ESWM), a neural network model that can rapidly construct a coherent spatial world model from sparse and disjoint episodic memories, enabling sample-efficient navigation and exploration.
