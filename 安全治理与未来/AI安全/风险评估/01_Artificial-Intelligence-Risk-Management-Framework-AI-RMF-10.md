# Artificial Intelligence Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0)

**来源**: [https://nvlpubs.nist.gov/nistpubs/ai/NIST.AI.100-1.pdf](https://nvlpubs.nist.gov/nistpubs/ai/NIST.AI.100-1.pdf)

**类型**: doc

## 简介

A voluntary framework developed by the U.S. National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) to manage risks and improve the trustworthiness of AI systems. It provides a flexible, four-function approach (Govern, Map, Measure, and Manage) to address AI risks throughout the AI lifecycle.
