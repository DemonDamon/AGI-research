# Artificial Intelligence and Its Implications for Future Suffering

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://longtermrisk.org/files/artificial-intelligence-and-its-implications-for-future-suffering.pdf](https://longtermrisk.org/files/artificial-intelligence-and-its-implications-for-future-suffering.pdf)

## 摘要

A 2016 paper from the Center on Long-Term Risk that discusses the possibility of a 'soft takeoff' or 'slow AI takeoff' scenario, which represents a more conservative prediction for the transition to advanced AI. It explores the implications for future suffering and the need for AI safety, ethics, and social implications research.
