# Bridging Today and the Future of Humanity: AI Safety in 2024 and Beyond

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.18114](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.18114)

## 摘要

A vision paper that presents a blueprint for an advanced human society and leverages this vision to guide current AI safety efforts. It outlines a roadmap of significant technological advancements and forecasts potential AI safety issues for each stage, offering a balanced, long-term perspective.
