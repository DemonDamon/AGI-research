# Should Artificial Intelligence Governance be Centralised?: Design Lessons from History

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://dl.acm.org/doi/abs/10.1145/3375627.3375857](https://dl.acm.org/doi/abs/10.1145/3375627.3375857)

## 摘要

An academic paper that explores the trade-offs of centralizing international AI governance, drawing lessons from the history of other international regimes. It concludes that the outcome depends on the exact design, suggesting a moderate, nuanced approach.
