# Worldwide AI ethics: A review of 200 guidelines and recommendations for AI governance

**链接**: [https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10591196/](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10591196/)

## 摘要

A meta-analysis of 200 global governance policies and ethical guidelines for AI, identifying 17 resonating principles and areas of consensus for future regulatory efforts.
