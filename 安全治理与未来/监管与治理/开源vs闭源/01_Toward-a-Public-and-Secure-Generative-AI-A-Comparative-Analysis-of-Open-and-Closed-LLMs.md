# Toward a Public and Secure Generative AI: A Comparative Analysis of Open and Closed LLMs

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.10603](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.10603)

## 摘要

A comparative analysis of open and closed Large Language Models (LLMs) focusing on security and ethical considerations, arguing for a regulatory and collaborative governance framework. It highlights the unique features and challenges of open-source systems in the context of public and secure generative AI.
