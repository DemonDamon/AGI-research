# Constitutional AI: Harmlessness from AI Feedback

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.08073](https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.08073)

## 摘要

The foundational paper by Anthropic introducing the Constitutional AI approach, which trains a harmless AI assistant through self-improvement using a set of principles (a "constitution") rather than extensive human labels for harmful outputs.
