**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.19852](https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.19852)

## 摘要

A comprehensive survey of AI alignment, covering concepts, methodologies, and practices, and highlighting the growing risks of misalignment as AI systems become more capable. It serves as an excellent starting point for understanding the field.
