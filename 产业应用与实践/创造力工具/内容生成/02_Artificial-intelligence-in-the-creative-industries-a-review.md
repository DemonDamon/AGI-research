# Artificial intelligence in the creative industries: a review

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10462-021-10039-7](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10462-021-10039-7)

## 摘要

A comprehensive review of the current state of the art in AI technologies and applications in the creative industries. It categorizes creative applications into five groups, including content creation, and differentiates between AI as a creative tool and a creator. The paper concludes that maximum benefit is derived when AI is human-centric, augmenting human creativity.
