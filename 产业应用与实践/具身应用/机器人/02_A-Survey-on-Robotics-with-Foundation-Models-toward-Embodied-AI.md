# A Survey on Robotics with Foundation Models: toward Embodied AI

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.02385](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.02385)

## 摘要

A comprehensive and up-to-date survey on the application of foundation models in robotics, with a focus on autonomous manipulation, high-level planning, and low-level control. It provides a structured overview of the field, including datasets, simulators, and benchmarks, and discusses critical challenges and future research directions for Embodied AI.
