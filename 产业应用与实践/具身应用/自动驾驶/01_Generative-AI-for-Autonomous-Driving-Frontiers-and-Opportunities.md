# Generative AI for Autonomous Driving: Frontiers and Opportunities

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.08854](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.08854)

## 摘要

A comprehensive survey on the emerging role of Generative AI (GenAI) across the autonomous driving stack, covering models like VAEs, GANs, Diffusion Models, and LLMs, and their applications in synthetic data, end-to-end driving, and cross-domain transfer to embodied AI.
