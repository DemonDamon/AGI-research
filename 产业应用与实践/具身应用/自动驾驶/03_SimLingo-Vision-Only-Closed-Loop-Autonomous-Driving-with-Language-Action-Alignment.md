# SimLingo: Vision-Only Closed-Loop Autonomous Driving with Language-Action Alignment

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.09594](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.09594)

## 摘要

A CVPR 2025 paper introducing SimLingo, a vision-language-action (VLA) model that unifies closed-loop driving, vision-language understanding, and language-action alignment using only camera input, demonstrating state-of-the-art performance on the CARLA simulator.
