# GAIA-2: A Controllable Multi-View Generative World Model for Autonomous Driving

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.20523](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.20523)

## 摘要

A technical paper introducing GAIA-2, a latent diffusion world model by Wayve, which unifies multi-agent interactions, fine-grained control, and multi-camera consistency for generating realistic and controllable driving simulations.
