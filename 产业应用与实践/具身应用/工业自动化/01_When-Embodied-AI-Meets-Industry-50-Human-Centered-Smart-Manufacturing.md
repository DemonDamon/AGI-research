# When Embodied AI Meets Industry 5.0: Human-Centered Smart Manufacturing

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/10909141/](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/10909141/)

## 摘要

A survey providing a general framework of smart manufacturing in the context of Industry 5.0, where embodied agents (robots, sensors, actuators) are carriers for Industrial AI (IndAI). It reviews key technologies like embodied perception, interaction, scheduling, and multi-mode large language models, and discusses open challenges and opportunities.
