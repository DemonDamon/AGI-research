# The Impact of Automation and Knowledge Workers on Employees' Outcomes: Mediating Role of Knowledge Transfer

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://www.researchgate.net/publication/358130623_The_Impact_of_Automation_and_Knowledge_Workers_on_Employees'_Outcomes_Mediating_Role_of_Knowledge_Transfer](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/358130623_The_Impact_of_Automation_and_Knowledge_Workers_on_Employees'_Outcomes_Mediating_Role_of_Knowledge_Transfer)

## 摘要

This study examines the mediating role of knowledge transfer between automation, knowledge workers, and employee outcomes (creativity and innovative performance). It finds support for the mediating role of knowledge transfer between automation and both employee creativity and innovative performance.
