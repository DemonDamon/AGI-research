# Scaling deep learning for materials discovery

**类型**: 论文 (Paper)

**链接**: [https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-023-06735-9](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-023-06735-9)

## 摘要

A landmark paper from DeepMind (Google) on using large-scale active learning and deep learning to discover millions of new materials, including 380,000 stable materials. This work led to the creation of the GNoME dataset.
